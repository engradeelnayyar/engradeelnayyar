### Advantages and Disadvantages of Private Label
#### Advantages
- Building Brand
- Once brand is build asset can be flipped
- Higher incomes in case product succeeded
#### Disadvantages
- Require higher costs
- Long Process
- Higher Risk Factor
- Risk of Insolvency

### Advantages and Disadvantages of Private Label

#### Advantages
- Low Risk Factor
- Require no high investment
#### Disadvantages
- Not higher profits
- No own brand building
